# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date: November 29, 2025**

We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how data is collected and used on this website.

## 1. Information We Collect

As a static Hugo blog, we collect minimal personal data. We do not require user registration or directly collect personally identifiable information (PII) like names or emails from visitors.

## 2. Cookies, Tracking, and Third-Party Services

We rely on third-party services which utilize cookies and tracking technologies to maintain site functionality and provide monetization:

* **Google Analytics (GA):** We use Google Analytics to collect data about website traffic, usage patterns, and user behavior (e.g., device type, geographical location, time spent on site). This data is collected and analyzed anonymously.
* **Google AdSense/Ad Manager:** We serve advertisements through Google. Google uses cookies (such as the DoubleClick DART cookie) to serve personalized ads based on your visit to this site and other websites. These cookies track your activity to serve interest-based ads.
* **Google Consent Management Platform (CMP):** For users within the European Economic Area (EEA) and the UK, we use Google's CMP to obtain your consent for the use of cookies for personalized advertising, as required by GDPR regulations.

## 3. Data Control and Your Choices

You have the option to manage or disable cookies through your web browser settings. Please note that disabling cookies may affect the functionality of this website and others you visit.

## 4. External Links

This website contains links to external websites (e.g., GitHub, related articles). We are not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of those external sites.

## 5. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or your data, please reach out to the site administrator.
